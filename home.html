Differentiation

Differentiation is a method used to compute the rate of change of a function f(x) with respect to its input x. This rate of change is known as the derivative of f with respect to x.


Integration

Integration, in mathematics, technique of finding a function g(x) the derivative of which, Dg(x), is equal to a given function f(x). This is indicated by the integral sign “∫,” as in ∫f(x), usually called the indefinite integral of the function.


Algebra

Algebra, branch of mathematics in which arithmetical operations and formal manipulations are applied to abstract symbols rather than specific numbers.


Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics that deals with the shape of objects, their spatial relations, and the properties of the space the objects are in.
